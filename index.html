About the Bay

door to door furniture

Blue Jay Bay is a furniture and appliance marketplace that operates within the Hopkins community. We purchase and sell furniture, appliances, and other large market items to Johns Hopkins students. Blue Jay Bay makes buying and selling easy by picking up and delivering items directly to your dorm or apartment!
